# Venue, Instructor, Teacher & Mentor Portal Features

## Overview
A comprehensive web-based portal designed for venues (restaurants, cafes, bars, parks, etc.), instructors, teachers, and mentors to manage their presence, content, events, classes, and interactions on the Vibe Connect platform.
